A frictionless loop

Drop. Shape.
Download.

  1. 01

    Bring the file

    Drop or choose WAV and MP3 files directly from your device. Inputs are capped at 64 MB before processing begins.

  2. 02

    Make the change

    Every heavy decode and final encode runs in a Web Worker, with visible progress and explicit cancellation.

  3. 03

    Take the result

    The finished Blob downloads from your browser. A cancelled or failed job creates no partial download.

Trust, stated precisely

Your audio is not the product.

Both surfaces process files locally and contain no upload or telemetry path. The extension can enforce more at the platform level; the hosted app is transparent about the difference.
